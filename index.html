FAQ's - Frequently Asked Questions

Why Computer Science?

I am interested in the field of Computer Science because it gives me the freedom to turn my imagination into reality. Anything I can envision, I can implement within this field. It also provides access to a vast range of problems and requirements, regardless of physical presence, making it indispensable in today's era of digitalization and innovation. Furthermore, all major domains such as law, security, finance, management, and marketing are deeply intertwined with Computer Science and rely on its advancements. The ability to develop and train tools, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), a technology that the world both fears and admires, reinforces the idea that the world is truly at our fingertips.

Why IIIT-Delhi?

I chose IIIT-Delhi for its cutting-edge, industry-oriented curriculum, which emphasizes projects and application-based learning, providing a strong foundation in the field from day one. The institute's rigorous, research-driven academics and learning-focused culture foster innovation and encourage students to think beyond limitations. Moreover, its strict anti-plagiarism policy and demanding academic workload push students out of their comfort zones, helping them grow both intellectually and professionally.

Why Computer Science + Social Science?

The name of the branch may seem misleading at first, but the curriculum offered by IIIT-Delhi under this specialization is thoughtfully designed and more balanced than many others. It not only covers essential mathematical and core Computer Science concepts but also includes subjects that enhance critical thinking, social perspective analysis, financial literacy, psychology, and management skills, fostering holistic development. These industry-oriented skills are invaluable when developing public-focused products and services, ensuring that financial and management constraints are effectively considered.
